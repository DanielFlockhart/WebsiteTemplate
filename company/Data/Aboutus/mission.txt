Our mission is to guide Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning in the direction that most positively impacts society. Whether it be gene editing or financial applications such as stock trading models, we believe AI has the capability to push the human race into a new era of progress, peace and prosperity. We also believe that with great power comes great responsibility, for this reason we take a strong stance on the philosophical perspectives of our work - If we percieve our work to have any negative implications, we will not continue developement.
